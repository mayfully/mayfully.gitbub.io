About Mayfully.

Hello! I am Seoyoung Seung or you can call me May. I have spent intensive years at Vancouver Film School, learning the skills and tools of a designer and practicing in maintaining a thorough process to create great user experience design and interactive design. I have had the opportunity to put all my qualifications and development skills. And now, I am currently working at Flitto inc in Seoul Korea.

Flitto

APP UX / UI Design

Flitto is a crowd-source translation platform based both app and web. I re-designed to dedicat along with two different user modes such as for requestors & translators. My goal was to develop UI to achieve the visual that is to bring a clean experience across the application. I also worked with developers to build out features from visuals.
